A collection of smaller research projects and experiments described on the CV:

- Effects of light wavelengths as insect attractants for pests and pollinators.
- Mouse training and mouse brain surgery protocols for brain calcium imaging studies.
- PCR to amplify the TERT gene (linked to ageing and oncogenesis) with SNP extraction.
- Effect of climate change on dispersal — Cox's Proportionality Hazard Model.
- Studied fly circadian rhythm changes after crossing GMR GAL-4 × UAS lines and analyzed resulting data.
